How does this work?

Label: runs on-device text recognition (OCR) on your photo and matches it to the database — no photo ever leaves your phone.

Barcode: builds a personal library. The first time you scan a bottle it won't know it — tap the right bottle once to link it, and every future scan of that barcode opens instantly.

If a feature isn't supported by your browser, you'll get a normal search box instead.
